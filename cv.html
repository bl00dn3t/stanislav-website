Stanislav Iordanov is a London-based visual artist, musician and performer. His work has recently shown at Neumarkt Theater, Zurich (2024), Cabaret Voltaire, Zurich (2023), Podium/K4, Oslo (2023), Ballhaus Ost, Berlin (2023), Fleetstreet Theater, Hamburg (2023), Kunstverein München (2022), Leech, London (2022), Thalia Theater, Hamburg (2022), Münchener Kammerspiele (2022), Saatchi Gallery, London (2021), Lothringer 13 Halle, Munich (2020), Le Bourgeois, London (2020), Prague Quadrennial (2019), Corner College, Zurich (2018), David Roberts Art Foundation, London (2017), Forde, Geneva (2017), Kunsthaus Glarus (2017) and the Victoria & Albert Museum, London (2017), among others.
